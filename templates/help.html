So How Stickers Work Anyway?

  1. Before the match starts, choose ten sticker sheets.
  2. Before the game starts, three sticker sheets are randomly chosen from the ten sheets.
  3. Every time a card instructs you to get tickets, click on the + above the Ticket Bucket-Bot
  4. Each sticker you can afford is automaticly highlighted by green. Otherwise it's red.
  5. To buy a sticker, just click on it. Remamber - names and arts are always free!
  6. Then, a text box would pop up. Write which of your card is now "stickered". No glue necessary!
  7. When a stickered card goes to a hidden zone (hand, deck, etc), click on the "x" button and the sticker would be back on the sheet.
